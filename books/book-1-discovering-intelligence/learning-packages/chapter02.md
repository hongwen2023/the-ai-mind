## Suggested Order

1. Read through the hand-calculated Rule 90 example.
2. Predict the first Notebook pattern before running it.
3. Complete two perturbation experiments.
4. Finish Common Illusions and Research Corner.
5. Complete the Understanding Audit without the chapter open.
6. Repeat reconstruction after seven days.

## Capability Milestone

After this package, the learner can:

- [ ] **Explain** how local updates generate a global pattern.
- [ ] **Predict** effects of state, rule, or update-order changes.
- [ ] **Build** and perturb a one-dimensional cellular automaton.
- [ ] **Read** a basic emergence claim and inspect its metric.
