## Overall Assessment

Chapter 1 is the strongest chapter produced in The AI Mind so far. It moves
beyond presenting AI content and begins to establish a learning-science method
in which AI is the working medium.

The chapter's central insight should remain:

> 理解建立答案之间的关系，使人能够解释、预测、重建并迁移。

## Strengths

- The subway disruption continues Prelude's map story without repeating it.
- The answer-card / transparent-machine analogy is clear and declares its
  limits.
- The two-rule mathematics example introduces the need for inductive bias
  without front-loading theory.
- Perturbation tests train prediction and debugging rather than API recall.
- The finance driver tree transfers the mechanism rather than substituting a
  finance noun into an AI example.
- Research Corner asks whether models learn transferable structure rather than
  merely summarizing papers.
- Explain, Predict, Reconstruct, Transfer forms a distinctive and reusable
  Understanding Audit.

## Required Minor Revisions

1. Add one sentence to the opening showing that the first traveler did not
   fail to remember; he failed to represent which information mattered.
2. State explicitly that compression does not equal truth.
3. Clarify that behavioral, causal, and internal-mechanism evidence reinforce
   one another rather than replace one another.
4. Tighten the final bridge into Chapter 2 around the question of how a complex
   world can arise from a small set of simple relationships.
