## Assessment

The brief meets the Golden Chapter Standard. It defines research as designing
evidence that separates competing explanations, rather than collecting a
plausible story for an observation.

## Draft Guidance

1. Preserve the Research Audit Framework: Observation, Alternatives,
   Prediction, Intervention, Measurement, Update.
2. Do not front-load full Bayesian inference, statistical testing, or causal
   inference.
3. Make the Shortcut Dataset lab distinguish explanations rather than merely
   display an accuracy drop.
4. Preserve the Earnings Miss Research Loop and its point-in-time predictions.
5. Treat Ablation as evidence, not automatic proof of a causal mechanism.
6. State that research improves discrimination among explanations rather than
   rushing toward a unique answer.
7. Add “support for a view is not proof of the view” to Common Illusions.
8. Bridge to Chapter 9 by requiring reconstruction of the Book I relationship
   map rather than recall of chapter titles.
